    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is it free to merge PDF files?
    Yes, our PDF merge tool is completely free to use with no hidden charges. You can merge as many PDFs as you need without any limitations.
    Are my PDF files secure?
    Absolutely! Your files are processed directly in your browser and never uploaded to our servers. This ensures maximum privacy and security for your documents.
    How many PDFs can I merge at once?
    You can merge up to 20 PDF files at once with a total size limit of 50MB. For larger documents, we recommend splitting them into smaller batches.
    What compression options are available?
    We offer four compression levels: No compression (best quality), Low compression (good quality), Medium compression (balanced), and High compression (smallest file size). You can also optimize images separately.
    Can I reorder pages after uploading?
    Yes, you can drag and drop files to reorder them before merging. The pages will appear in the merged document in the order you specify.
